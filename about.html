About Me

Designing with
intention.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer and developer with over eight years of experience crafting thoughtful digital experiences. My work lives at the intersection of visual design, front-end development, and strategic thinking.

I believe the best design is invisible — it solves problems elegantly and puts the human experience first. Whether working on a brand identity system, a complex web application, or a simple landing page, I bring the same rigorous attention to detail to every project.

My background spans both the analytical and the creative. I studied Communication Design at Parsons School of Design, and before going independent, I worked at agencies in New York and London helping global clients like Agency A, Studio B, and Brand C find their visual voice.

When I'm not at my desk, you'll find me exploring museums, shooting 35mm film, or obsessing over the typography in restaurant menus.
